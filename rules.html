RULES FOR REGISTRATION



Gaming Structure

1. The game involves a story divided into 20 levels with some surprise bonus levels with extra rewards. The entire game spans over 2 days (48 hours)
2. Each level revolves around a sub-plot of the story with some technical challenges attached to it.
3. You must try to solve the challenge to get to the next level, where the next part of the story will be revealed.
4. There is no limitation on number of attempts made by a user.
5. There is a unique solution to every question.
6. Help can be asked in the comment section of the gaming platform.
7. Hint for the questi as featured comment in the comment section.
8. Percentile and Current level will be shown in the progress bar.
9. Users posting direcons will be provided after certain period of time. Same will be displayedt answers to the comment section will be banned to use the comment section (* may be banned from game, if answers of higher levels).




Registration

1. The registration for the event will start from 11th October 2017.
2. Registrations for the event can be done with the help of signup option on the navigation bar.
3. Automatic redirection to game page after successful login.
4. Game page can also be accessed from the homepage.
5. Notifications for the event will be sent to the registered email prior to the event.




Awards for achievements

1. Prizes worth 50k
2. Goodies for lucky registration participants.
3. Grand prizes(Cash prizes) at final level includes :

  • 1st prize - INR 7000
  • 2nd prize - INR 5000
  • 3rd prize - INR 3000
4. The top 2 winners of each level will be awarded with goodies (not cash) on subject to consideration that they are not among the winners in any of the previous level.
5. Prizes for the individual levels(excluding bonus levels) include:
  • 1st prize-16GB Pendrive
  • 2nd prize-T-shirt
6. Prizes for the bonus levels(goodies) include:
  • 1st prize-Bluetooth Speaker
  • 2nd prize-16GB Pendrive
  • 3rd prize-T-shirt
